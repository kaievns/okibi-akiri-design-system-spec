01 · shin

Six convictions, settled

Every rule on this site derives from these. If a decision isn't covered, derive it from here — don't invent sideways.

予算

Conserve salience

Total emphasis is fixed. Every element lit dims every other. Under ~15% of any view carries colour at rest.

明

Luminance leads, hue is rationed

Hierarchy rides lightness and weight. Hue marks a few nominal categories, kept muted.

燠

One ember, kept sacred

The single warm accent means "here / act" — cursor, focus, primary action. Never a token, never a link, never decoration.

静

Calm over clever

Near-monochrome, low saturation, muted ground. Calm-at-rest is a hard requirement, not a preference.

恒

The rule is constant

Predictability is the contract. Transitions are cuts; state changes fire on intentional boundaries, never on drift.

重

Redundant where it counts

Anything that must be told apart carries a second channel beyond hue — shape, weight, underline style.
